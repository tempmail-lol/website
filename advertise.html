Advertise With Us!

Want to advertise with us? Now you can! Advertisements will appear on the front page of our website.

We do have some rules, so please look before you make an advertisement:

Advertisements may not:
- Advertise competing websites
- Promote the sale of illegal goods/services
- Show adult content
- Infringe upon copyright
- Anything else that's illegal

If your ad isn't approved, we'll let you know why!
If a bid is made and the buyer does not pay, the second-highest bid will be accepted instead.
Failure to pay the first winning bid will result in a permanent ban from advertising on our services.

How it works

At the time listed on the "Bid ends" column in the table below, the first place winner will have 24 hours (UTC time) to pay for the advertisement.
If that person does not pay, the second place winner will have 24 hours AFTER the first place winner's time is up to pay for their advertisement.

Bids end Sunday at 11:59 PM UTC time. The advertisement will go live the following Wednesday at 11:59 PM UTC

We accept both Credit/Debit (through Stripe) and Cryptocurrency (through Coinbase) for payment.

Bid prices are updated every time a new bid is processed (you will receive an email once your bid has been processed).

Specifications

You can have HTML on your advertisement. It can't include scripts, flashing colors, or anything that messes with the site, but you can animate it.

Ads will be at most 800px wide, and will be shrunk depending on the device screen width.

Ads are 128px tall and will have a black border around them.
