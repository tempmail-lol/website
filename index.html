What is TempMail?

TempMail is a temporary email address generator. It generates temporary email addresses to use for other websites without giving away your real email address. TempMail is also known as 10minutemail, fake email, burner email, and throwaway email. Free Wi-Fi, forum owners, and websites require that you signup with an email address before allowing you to access content.
