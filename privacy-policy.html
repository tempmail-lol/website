Privacy Policy

last modified June 20th of 2022

Data we collect

We collect a minimal amount of information on our users. This includes the following:

  1. HTTP logs which may contain your User-Agent, IP address, HTTP headers, and full-URL.
  2. The email address generated by visiting our services.
  3. An authentication token generated which gives you access to your temporary email.
  4. A number of how many current users are connected to our services (displayed publicly).

We cannot guarantee that the above information is stored, only that it may be stored.

We do not match IP addresses to any email address generated; however, Cloudflare, an unaffiliated service which we use to proxy our traffic, may collect this information, please review their privacy policy at the following link: https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/.

Email addresses and tokens generated are ephemeral, and will be erased after ten (10) minutes of last access, or will be erased regardless of last access time in one (1) hour after the initial email generation.
The main page, https://tempmail.lol, will access the server every five (5) seconds.

Data sharing

We do not share our data with our users knowingly. We require users connect to our site using encryption, which is managed automatically by modern browsers. Emails sent to our server are sent unencrypted over the Internet, but encrypted before being sent to users.

Proof of ownership

We do not allow for any kind of registration on our platform; therefore, information regarding accounts and their ownership is non-existent.

Data Usage

The following data is sent through third-party servers which are not affiliated with our services:

  1. Cloudflare
    • Cloudflare may collect information which may be present in logs:
      • IP address
      • Ray-ID
      • HTTP method
      • HTTP version
      • Website host (such as "tempmail.lol")
      • Full URL (such as "https://tempmail.lol/about.html")
      • Query strings
      • User-Agent
      • ASN (Autonomous System Number)
      • Country
    • Please read the Cloudflare privacy policy to learn more: https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/
  2. Oracle Cloud
    • We use the Oracle Cloud platform to host our services. Please see their privacy policy for what information they may collect on their infrastructure.

Erasure

Any logs stored on our end, such as IP addresses, browsers, HTTP headers, and User-Agents, are deleted at midnight (UTC time) every Sunday.

Your email address that is generated if you do not access our website within 10 minutes of it being generated. This is done automatically.

Users may request data erasure by emailing erasure@exploding.email. Please wait at most two weeks before the request is made. You may or may not receive a response email.

No user data is kept for more than three (3) weeks worth of time.
