Configure Private Domain

You will need TempMail Plus or TempMail Ultra to use private domains

Please add the following two records to your domain

Record Type Name Content TTL Priority
TXT _tmpml one minute 10
MX @ mx.exploding.email one minute N/A

Note that it may take up to 48 hours for your registrar to update its records.

Save this token on your computer, you will need it to access your emails:

If you lose the token, visit this page to change your domain records.

After adding these records, check your custom domain emails at this page.
